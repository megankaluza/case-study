Workflow

Showcasing a color wheel that explains the meaning between color and emotion.

Why are we attracted to certain colors and what is the meaning behind their shades and hues? As designers we use colors to create a certain mood or feeling for our target audience.

Inspiration

I started by sketching low-fidelity wireframes in my notebook that would become effective across multiple screen sizes.

As I was researching, I was inspired by neurons and molecules, while mixed with different colors. I wanted to connect this type of energy to emotion.

I chose my typography and color palette based on the inspiration I found while researching.
