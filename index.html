Introduction

Showcasing a color wheel that explains the meaning between color and emotion.

Why are we attracted to certain colors and what is the meaning behind their shades and hues?

As designers we use colors to create a certain mood or feeling for our target audience.

While going through this process as a UX/UI designer, I had to break it down into it's simplest form. I first apporached this project by using my notebook to sketch wireframes.

Next, I started researching online and creating a color palette on Sketch.

I started creating medium fidelity mock-ups, using webpage, tablet, and mobile templates to make the design scaleable.

During Week 2, I learned how to design logos and icons, so I focused my energy on creating an inspiration board with my custom designs.

Week 3, I learned the concept of atomic design.
